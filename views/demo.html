The Netbattler

This is a brief demo on a megaman net bsttle inspired html5 game.

Rules
Tap a spot to mave the character to that spot.
The chacter will begin charging a shot automatically.
Tap on the chacter to unleash the shot.
Once the routines menu is full tap to get a list of your avaible routines.
You many load up to three routines into the character.
To execute a routine tap the small circle with the routines symbol. Begin
